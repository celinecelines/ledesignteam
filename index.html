Who

Founded by Céline Semaan Vernon in 2013 in New York, Le Design Team is a Design Creative Organization that brings projects to life by way of user-centred design, user experience, experiential design, and creative direction. Céline is an MIT Media Lab Director‘s Fellow, serves on the Board of Directors of AIGA NY in charge of events and programming bridging the gap between design, social change and human rights. Her fashion activism label Slow Factory produces meaningful accessories which have been worn by Alicia Keys and Pharrell Williams and is sold at the MoMA store NYC.
